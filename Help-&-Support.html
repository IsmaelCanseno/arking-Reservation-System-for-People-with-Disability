Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about EZIO:

How do I verify my disability status?

To verify your disability status, navigate to your profile settings and upload a clear photo or scan of your official disability documentation (such as a disabled parking permit or doctor's certification). Our team typically reviews submissions within 24-48 hours.

What if someone parks in my reserved spot?

If your reserved spot is occupied when you arrive:

  • Use the "Report Issue" button in your reservation details
  • Our support team will contact the parking facility immediately
  • We'll help you find the nearest available alternative spot
  • You'll receive a full refund or credit for the inconvenience
Can I cancel or modify a reservation?

Yes, you can cancel or modify reservations up to 1 hour before your scheduled arrival time without penalty. To make changes:

  1. Go to "My Reservations" in the app
  2. Select the reservation you want to change
  3. Choose "Modify" or "Cancel"
  4. Follow the prompts to complete your changes
How does the real-time parking availability work?

Our real-time system uses a combination of technologies:

  • IoT sensors installed in parking spaces detect occupancy
  • Parking facility staff can update status manually when needed
  • User reports help verify availability when sensors aren't present
  • Our algorithm predicts availability based on historical patterns

This multi-source approach ensures the most accurate information possible.

Is my personal and payment information secure?

Absolutely. EZIO uses bank-level security measures:

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Payment information is processed through PCI-compliant systems
  • Disability documentation is stored securely and only accessible to authorized verification staff
  • We never sell or share your personal information

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Having trouble with the app? Try these solutions:

No Parking Spots Showing
  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify your location services are enabled
  • Adjust your search radius in settings
App Crashes or Freezes
  • Close and restart the app
  • Check for app updates
  • Restart your device
  • Reinstall the app if issues persist
Payment Problems
  • Verify your card details are correct
  • Check with your bank for holds
  • Try an alternative payment method
  • Ensure sufficient funds are available
Not Receiving Notifications
  • Check notification permissions in app settings
  • Verify your device isn't in Do Not Disturb mode
  • Ensure background app refresh is enabled

Still need help? Contact our support team for personalized assistance.
