Security & Privacy Policies (Online Parking for Disabled)

Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our privacy policy is designed to be clear and easy to understand, outlining how we handle your personal information. It covers:

  • Data Collection:

    Specifically, we explain what data we collect, including:

    • Account information (name, contact details).
    • Disability verification documents (if required).
    • Vehicle information (license plate, type).
    • Location data (parking searches, parking sessions).
    • Usage data (app activity, preferences).
    • Information needed for payment processing.

    We emphasize that disability-related information is treated with the utmost sensitivity.

  • Data Usage:

    We detail how your data is used:

    • To facilitate parking reservations and payments.
    • To verify disability status for eligibility.
    • To provide personalized parking recommendations.
    • To improve app functionality and user experience.
    • To communicate important updates and notifications.
    • To prevent fraud.

    We assure users that their data will not be used for unauthorized purposes.

  • Data Protection:

    We explain the measures we take to secure your data:

    • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest.
    • Secure server infrastructure with access controls.
    • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.
    • Data anonymization where possible.
  • User Rights:

    We outline your rights regarding your data:

    • Right to access your data.
    • Right to rectify inaccurate data.
    • Right to erase your data (subject to legal obligations).
    • Right to restrict data processing.
    • Right to data portability.
    • Right to object to data processing.
    • How to exercise these rights.

We provide a link to the full privacy policy for detailed information: Privacy Policy

Security Measures

We take the security of your data seriously. We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure:

  • Encryption: All sensitive data, including disability information and payment details, is encrypted using industry-standard encryption protocols.
  • Access Controls: Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only, and access is logged and monitored.
  • Secure Servers: Our servers are housed in secure data centers with physical and logical security measures in place.
  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct regular security audits and penetration testing to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.
  • Data Minimization: We only collect and retain the data necessary to provide our services.
  • Disability Data Protection: Special protections are in place for disability related data.
  • Payment Security: We utilize trusted third-party payment processors that are PCI DSS compliant.

We continuously monitor and update our security measures to stay ahead of emerging threats.
