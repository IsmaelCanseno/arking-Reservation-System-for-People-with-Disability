About PRSPD

App Description

This app simplifies finding and reserving accessible parking spaces for individuals with disabilities. Our mission is to help those in need of available parking spaces for the disabled, ensuring they can easily locate and secure suitable parking. Our target audience is the physically disabled, aiming to enhance their mobility and independence.

App Features and Benefits

  • Real-time Parking Availability: Instantly see available disabled parking spots near your location.
  • Advanced Search Filters: Filter parking spaces by specific accessibility features (e.g., width, proximity to entrances).
  • Reservation System: Secure your parking spot in advance to guarantee availability.
  • Navigation Assistance: Get turn-by-turn directions to your reserved parking space.
  • Disability Verification: Securely upload and verify disability documentation for eligibility.
  • Payment Integration: Conveniently pay for parking through the app.
  • User Reviews and Ratings: View and contribute to parking space reviews and ratings.
  • Reminders and Notifications: Receive alerts for upcoming reservations and parking time limits.
  • Reporting Feature: Allows users to report misuse of disabled parking spots.
